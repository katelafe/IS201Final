About Kate

"Wherever life plants you, bloom with grace"
BRIDAL EXPO      |       WEDDING PLANNING      |       TRAVEL INSPIRATION





Passion

As a young girl, I developed a real admiration of flowers. As I have spent the last several years working in the wedding industry, that interest has grown into a passion. I love to make all things as a beautiful as possible and strive to expand my own skills through inspiration and experience. My favorite song of all time is "La Vie En Rose", which is french for "life in pink". I try to view the world as a beautiful place or as they say, "in pink".

My love for people has led me to a lifelong aspiration to create and deepen human relations. In my studies of Human Resource Management, I have learned the importance of appreciating differences in people. Each individual has their own set of strengths and weaknesses, and I try to encourage others to focus on strengthening their strengths.

One of my main passions in life is to improve situations. I love to make things pretty, organized, and functional. Let me help you make you dreams a beautiful and clean lifestyle come true! Inquire for my professional organizing help here

Crisp Bridal Expo



This is my team that helped me plan and execute a bridal expo hosted by our company, Crisp Catering. We worked with over 20 different bridal vendors from all over Utah and collaborated with them to design an experience for over 300 brides in Utah County.

Wedding Planning

I developed a love for floral design and made this rose bouquet for a friend's wedding. Floral details are my favoirte way of creating a delicate and feminine look.

This elegant venue is at Sleepy Ridge Golf Course in Orem, UT. We teamed up with Moonlight Lighting Co. to create a magical space with the use of drapery, twinkle lights, and white linens.

Travel inspiration



From Peru, I learned about the beauty in the usage of brick and delicious food. This inspired many elements in the Crisp Bridal Expo.







In Paris, I was inspired by the street music, the food, and the lights. Paris is a very artistic and beautiful place. This inspired the lighting at the Sleepy Ridge venue, and inspired many food choices such as fondue, croissant sandwiches, and other pastries.
